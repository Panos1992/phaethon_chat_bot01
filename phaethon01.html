About PHAETHON

PHAETHON is a European Center of Excellence dedicated to advancing renewable energy technologies and sustainable energy solutions. Our interdisciplinary team brings together experts from across Europe to address the urgent challenges of climate change and energy transition.

Through collaborative research and innovation, we aim to develop and implement cutting-edge renewable energy systems, enhance energy efficiency, and accelerate the transition to a low-carbon economy across Europe and beyond.
